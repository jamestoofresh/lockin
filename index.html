🌅 Summer Routine

Morning Reset (8:00 AM – 10:00 AM)

  • Make your bed
  • Cold water face splash
  • Brush teeth & clean face
  • Drink water
  • Stretch + workout
  • Cool shower + outfit
  • Balanced breakfast

Midday (11:00 AM – 3:00 PM)

  • Physical activity
  • Summer reading
  • Skill learning
  • Connect with friends
  • Healthy lunch
  • Recreational screen time (1 hr max)

Evening (4:00 PM – 7:00 PM)

  • Optional evening workout
  • Wash up + rinse
  • Help with chores
  • Journal or family time

Night Wind Down (8:00 PM – 10:30 PM)

  • Evening walk
  • Limit screen time
  • Shower + gratitude journal
  • Lights out by 10:30 PM

📚 School Routine

Before School

  • Wake up at 6:30 AM
  • Brush, clean face, hydrate
  • Pack lunch/snack
  • Outfit & review school notes

After School

  • Unpack and clean up
  • Homework (start by 4 PM)
  • Break + healthy snack
  • Review goals or projects

Night Routine

  • Limit phone after 9 PM
  • School prep for next day
  • Shower + skincare
  • Sleep by 10 PM

🎄 Holiday Routine

Morning

  • Wake by 9:00 AM
  • Drink water + stretch
  • Holiday breakfast
  • Plan fun/family activity

Daytime

  • Chill day — play games, relax
  • Journal thoughts or gratitude
  • Post or edit content
  • Short walk or movement

Evening

  • Family movie or dinner
  • Hot shower
  • Lights out by 11 PM
